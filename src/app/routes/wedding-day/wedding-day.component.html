Ceremony & Reception

MaryBud Farm

Antioch, IL

MaryBud Farms was established in 2015 by Morgan’s parents, Mark and Maril MacDonald, to preserve the legacy of a place for family and friends to gather. It's named in memory of Morgan’s grandparents, "Bud" and Mary Gagen.

The property is about creating a community around what’s possible and where we’re going, rooted in the core values of key people in our lives. This makes "the farm" a natural destination for us to start our lives together, surrounded by the community that has played an integral part of who we are.

Attire

Suggested attire is relaxed formal (jacket optional) with lawn appropriate shoes as the ceremony and reception will be outdoors.


We'll be outside! Wear shoes you can dance in the grass in such as, flats, sandals, or cowboy boots.

Be smitten, not bitten! We’ll be handing out bug spray, so wear clothes that are bug spray friendly, and avoid wearing scents that would attract bugs.

For inspiration… ladies’ suggested attire is summer cocktail dresses, maxi dresses, nice blouse and dress pants. Men’s suggested attire is dress shirt and dress pants. Tie and jacket are optional.

Please no jeans, shorts, sneakers, flip flops, or heels.
