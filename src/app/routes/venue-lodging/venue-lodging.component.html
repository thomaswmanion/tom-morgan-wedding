Transportation

Flying in:

O’Hare International Airport (ORD)

Approx. 45 minutes

Milwaukee’s General Mitchell International Airport (MKE)

Approx. 50 minutes

Airport to Hotel:

You can Uber (~$50-$60) or take a cab (~$70+tip) from the airport to the hotel. 

When returning to the airport, we suggest to arrange transportation ahead of time as ridesharing options are still limited in this area.

Hotel to Wedding:

We have arranged shuttle buses to run to and from MaryBud Farms. If staying at the hotel, please use the shuttle as parking is limited at the farm.

Hotel to Wedding:

We will have limited parking at the property for local guests only. The parking entrance is at the south end of the property, off of Edwards Road. Directions will be emailed one week before the wedding to those who have indicated they will be driving on their RSVP form.
